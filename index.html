JOINING WITH US AT PHILLY EVENTS

The 2023 FESTIVALS —- thank you local business for your support!

Our 2023 Philly Festival will take place through out the year. All information for your application for the Philly Festival are submitted on this page. After applying business will need to send photos and/or their website or social media pages for jurying.

Booths for each event cost $250 for a busniess. You can select (and pay for) a double booth in the application process if you’d like larger space. Our festival provides generous space for all business with most spaces being “corner spaces” for optimal room. Each venfor are responsible for their own booth set-up with white EZ-Up type of canopies being typical for the display.

As part of the application you will be redirected to PayPal to pay for your booth fees, using a credit card or your PayPal account. Cancellations must be submitted by email to phillyfestival@gmail.com, hopefully from the email account you used when you applied.
